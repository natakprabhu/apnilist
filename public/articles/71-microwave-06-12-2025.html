How to Choose a Microwave Under ₹10,000

Start with capacity: 17–20 L works for singles and couples; 20–23 L suits small families. Prefer solo models from reliable brands with strong service in your city, simple controls you’ll use daily, and a design that fits your counter. For baking and grilling, upgrade to a convection microwave or OTG once your budget allows.
