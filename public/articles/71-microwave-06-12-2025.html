Buying Advice

If your goal is just reheating and making tea/popcorn, a Solo Microwave like the Samsung 23L is the best value. However, if you occasionally bake, look for "Convection" in the title. Under ₹10,000, Godrej and Haier offer the most affordable convection entries.
