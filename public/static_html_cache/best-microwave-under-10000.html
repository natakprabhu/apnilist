10 Best Microwaves Under ₹10,000 in India (2025)

By ApniList Team
Updated on 12/12/2025

Looking for a reliable microwave oven under ₹10,000? This list covers the best solo models from top brands with real-world pros, cons, and ideal use cases so you can decide in minutes.

In this price range you mostly get solo microwaves that handle reheating, defrosting and basic cooking very well. If you bake and grill occasionally, you can still pair a solo microwave with an OTG later instead of stretching your budget now.

#1

IFB 20 L Solo Microwave Oven

Best Overall Under ₹10,000
Rating: ★★★★☆ (4.5/5)

A dependable 20 L solo microwave for 2–3 member families with multiple power levels, reliable heating, and strong IFB service support.

Typical price: ₹8,500–₹9,500

Pros

  • Even heating with multiple power levels.
  • Good build quality and after-sales service.
  • Ideal size for small families and bachelors.

Cons

  • No convection or grill mode.
  • Design is functional rather than premium.
#2

Best for Reheating
Rating: ★★★★☆ (4.4/5)

LG’s 20 L solo microwave focuses on consistent reheating, stable performance, and easy-to-use controls, perfect if you mostly warm food, drinks, and leftovers.

Typical price: ₹8,000–₹9,000

#3

Samsung 23 L Solo Microwave Oven

Best for Larger Bowls
Rating: ★★★★☆ (4.3/5)

A slightly larger 23 L solo microwave that fits big plates and bowls, ideal for families that batch cook and reheat frequently.

  • Spacious interior for large dishes.

Cons

How to Choose a Microwave Under ₹10,000

Focus on capacity (17–20 L for singles/couples, 20–23 L for small families), a trusted brand with strong service, and simple controls you will actually use daily. Under ₹10,000, solo models offer the best value; for baking and grilling, upgrade to a convection unit later when budget allows.
