Whether you are looking for a morning detox companion or a machine to handle tough greens, the 2025 juicer market offers excellent options ranging from high-tech cold press machines to efficient budget-friendly centrifugal models.

1. Kuvings B1700 (Best Cold Press)

Why we love it

Kuvings remains the gold standard for cold press juicers in the Indian market. It features a patented wide feeding tube that fits whole apples, saving prep time. The motor is powerful (240W) and silent.

Pros: High yield, Silent motor, 10-year warranty

Cons: Premium price tag

2. Sujata Powermatic Plus (Best Durability)

Why we love it

A legendary appliance in Indian households, known for being virtually indestructible. The 900-watt motor destroys everything. It is incredibly fast and efficient for families who want quick juice.

Pros: Very fast, Affordable, Durable

Cons: Loud operation, Juice oxidizes quickly

Buying Guide: Cold Press vs. Centrifugal

Before buying, it is crucial to decide between the two main types of juicers:

Masticating (Cold Press)

Uses a slow-turning screw to crush produce. It retains more nutrients and enzymes because no heat is generated. Best for leafy greens and health enthusiasts.

Centrifugal (Fast Juicers)

Uses a fast-spinning blade. It is quick and cheaper but can heat up the juice slightly, reducing shelf life. Best for hard fruits like carrots and apples.
